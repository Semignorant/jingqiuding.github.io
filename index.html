Jingqiu Ding

I am a 5th year phd student of Computer Science at ETH Zurich, where I am fortunately supervised by Prof.David Steurer.

I work on algorithmic statistics, with the focus on robust low rank estimation algorithms based on sum-of-squares hierarchy or low degree spectral methods. Recently, I am applying these algorithmic ideas to the differential privacy.
