I am a Postdoc in Computer Science department at ETH Zurich. Before this, I got my computer science PhD in ETH Zurich, fortunately advised by Prof. David Steurer.

I work on computational complexity in machine learning problems, with a focus on robust and differentially private random graph estimation. Recently I have also become interested in diffusion models. In Fall 2024 I was fortunate to visit Prof. Sitan Chen.
